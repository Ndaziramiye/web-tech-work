What is art like in Rwanda?

Youth center walls, corrugated tin fences and shop stalls across the country sport
messages of inspiration, whimsy, and activism from local painters,
each adding a beauty mark in a public space. Art in Rwanda isn't limited
to canvases or galleries. Throughout the country, artists express themselves through whimsy, color, and imaginative...
Rwanda is known for its diverse and vibrant arts scene which includes traditional
and contemporary art form. Traditional art often includes crafts such as
basket weaving,ceramics, and wood carving. Imigongo a unique cow dung art,
is also a significant part of Rwanda's cultural heritage.
